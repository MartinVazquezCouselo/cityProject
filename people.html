People

Some of the most important people who were born in Los Angeles are:

Leonardo DiCaprio

About Him

The actor initially rose to fame with roles in the 1990s such as What's Eating Gilbert Grape, Romeo + Juliet and Titanic. He went on to star in Catch Me If You Can, Blood Diamond, The Departed and Inception. He won an Academy Award for Best Actor for his role as Hugh Glass in the 2015 film The Revenant.

Curiosities

He made his big screen debut playing the character Josh in the 1991 film Critters 3. His roles as Howard Hughes in The Aviator and Jordan Belfort in The Wolf of Wall Street earned him Golden Globe Awards for Best Actor.

Cameron Boyce

About Him

Actor, dancer, and artist who rose to fame for his roles as a child actor in Grown Ups and Eagle Eye. He also landed starring roles as Luke Ross on the popular Disney series Jessie and as Conor on Gamer's Guide to Pretty Much Everything. He played Carlos de Vil in the Disney television movies Descendants, Descendants 2 and Descendants 3.

Curiosities

He rose in the world of breakdancing with the dance group X-Mob. In 2019, he appeared as Zack on HBO's Mrs. Fletcher.
